My Henna Experience

My first henna experience was actually planning and implementing one for my hall residents in 2020!
The workshop was an hour long, repeated once to accommodate many residents while still adhering to safe-distancing measures.
It started off with a short educational talk by a student expert, followed by the henna drawing activity itself.
Many of us practiced drawing on paper first, then on blown-up latex gloves. We did patch testing before we decorated our hands with the henna paste.
It was a fun to see everyone having an opportunity to express their creativity!
